Coursing Information.




The courses you take at lane can greatly affect what profession you're choose to pursue. However, don't stress too much about it. The first two years at Lane are more about "weeding out" the classes you don't want and watering the academics that you find interesting!

Depending on what you wanna do, there are different curriculums you can take. However, keep in mind that- depending on the one you choose- you might lose certain options that you can fit in your schedule. For example, Computer Sciencerequires you to have 4 total credits of CS classes in order to graduate. This means that you're gonna have less space in your schedule for extra fine art classes like Guitar or Piano.
